# Rangolitico

## About

Dwelve into the breakfast dungeon, solve puzzles and seek the Super Legendary Recipe, alone or with a friend! Collaboration is an essential tool in order to beat the trials and tribulations that await inside this mysterious underground keep, where every piece of food has a mind of its own. Face fire spewing dragonfruit turrets, onion beetles and many delicious monstrocities in this cooperative multiplayer experience for up to two players!
